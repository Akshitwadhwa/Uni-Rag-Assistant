Internship Guidelines for Undergraduate Students

Students are encouraged to complete internships after the fourth semester once they have completed the core prerequisites in programming, data structures, and basic software engineering.

Credit-bearing internships are normally scheduled during the summer term after semester 6 or during semester 7 as part of industry practice. A sixth-semester internship during regular class weeks is not automatically permitted because it may conflict with attendance requirements.

If a student wishes to pursue an internship in the sixth semester, the student must obtain prior approval from:

- the faculty advisor
- the head of department
- the training and placement office

Approval is granted only when:

- the internship is directly relevant to the program outcomes
- the student has no active backlogs
- the student's CGPA is at least 7.5
- a plan exists to satisfy attendance and internal assessment requirements

All students must submit an internship completion certificate, supervisor evaluation, and internship report within two weeks of completion.
