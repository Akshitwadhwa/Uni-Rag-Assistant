Scholarship and Financial Aid FAQ

The university offers merit scholarships, need-based fee support, and special assistance for students representing the university in national-level sports and research competitions.

Merit Scholarship:
Students with a CGPA of 8.5 or above and no disciplinary action are eligible for a tuition fee waiver of up to 30 percent for the following academic year. Continuation of the scholarship requires the student to maintain a CGPA of at least 8.0.

Need-Based Fee Support:
Students whose annual household income is below INR 3,00,000 may apply for partial fee support. Applications must include an income certificate, fee receipt, and a statement of purpose. The financial aid committee reviews applications once every semester.

Research and Innovation Grant:
Students who publish in indexed conferences or journals with faculty supervision may apply for reimbursement of registration fees up to INR 25,000 subject to approval by the dean of research.

Sports Assistance:
Students representing the university in inter-university or national competitions may receive attendance relaxation and travel reimbursement according to the sports council policy.
